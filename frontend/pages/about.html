How It Works

1. Location Analysis

Start by selecting your village or locality. We use area-specific soil data and agricultural patterns to provide initial recommendations.

2. Visual Assessment

Answer simple questions about your soil's appearance, texture, and drainage to get more accurate recommendations.

3. Lab Report Entry

For the most accurate results, enter your soil test report data including NPK values and pH levels.

4. Get Recommendations

Receive detailed crop recommendations, fertilizer plans, and farming guidance tailored to your specific conditions.
